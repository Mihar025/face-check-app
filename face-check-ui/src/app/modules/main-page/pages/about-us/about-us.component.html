Our Story

Facecheck was founded in 2022 by a team of technology and HR professionals who saw firsthand how outdated payroll systems were holding businesses back.

After experiencing the frustration of manual timesheets, error-prone calculations, and lack of insights, our founders set out to build a solution that would revolutionize workforce management.

Today, Facecheck serves over 100 businesses across multiple industries, helping them save time, reduce errors, and gain valuable insights into their workforce productivity and finances.
